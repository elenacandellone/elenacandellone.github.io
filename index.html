About me

Ph.D. candidate at the department of Methodology and Statistics of Utrecht University. I have a background as a statistical physicist, with a Double M.Sc. degree in Physics of Complex Systems at Politecnico di Torino and Université Paris Cité. I'm interested in Network Science and Computational Social Science. My research is focussed on modeling complex social systems and describing the underlying patterns that emerge from the collective behavior of individuals.
